About Me

As a touring musician, on the road you really have no other option then to be empathetic toward your bandmates. You spend so much time in close quarters with one another, you learn to always consider someone else's point of view before making a decision that may affect them. I'd like to think this was my first foray into User Experience Design. Stuck on a bus for 3 months at a time, trying to find the minimum viable product that would keep us all sane.
